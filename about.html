Our Journey

A Path to Healing

Sopanam Sattva is more than a wellness center; it is a story of transformation. Founded by Sreedevi and Jinan, it represents over 25 years of dedication to Yoga and Nature Living.

Sreedevi's Story

"Till 2000, I was suffering lots of health issues, severe asthma, and heart issues. Then I realized medicine alone would not help me—I had to change my lifestyle."

Sreedevi embarked on a 41-day raw diet journey which brought remarkable results. This personal revelation led to a deep study of Yoga, Pranayama, Meditation, and Food. Today, she teaches people not just what to eat, but how, when, and how much to eat.

"Small changes in lifestyle bring big results. Food is medicine. Health is our wealth."

Our Village

A Sanctuary in Nature

Located in the serene village of Chemmanda, near sages' abode Irinjalakuda, Sopanam Sattva is nestled amidst lush green paddy fields and diverse flora. The air here is pure, and the silence is sacred.

Every structure is built with eco-friendly laterite stones and clay tiles to breathe, keeping the interiors cool and harmonic with the earth.
